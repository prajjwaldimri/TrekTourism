Satopanth Trek

Satopanth is a secluded and untouched lake that lies close to the hustle-bustle of the holy town of BadrinathHidden amidst the most impressive ranges of the Garhwal region, this bright green lake exudes grace and serenity. It makes for a perfect detour from a pilgrimage to Badrinath.Although located just 22 km from the popular Badrinath, the trail has several hidden treasures.The short distance of Satopanth Lake Trek is well-compensated with a frequent change in sceneries.

Saptkund Trek

Saptkund (seven lakes) is a group of mysterious lakes nestled in the foothills of gigantic Nanda ghunti peak. The lakes are located in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand at an altitude of 5000 meters. The trek to the lakes is DIFFICULTE one, but amassing with flora and fauna, pristine landscapes and unpredictable Himalayan weather.

Roopkund Trek

The Rookund Lake is captivating, adventurous and triggers fascination due to the enigma that goes about this mysterious lake. The folklore of locals, the scenic Himalayan landscape and abundance of flora & fauna makes trek to Roopkund much recommended for hikers and nature lovers, as well and for those who loves to unravel the unknown. Hindus have deep respect for Roopkund Lake because of its location and coz of the skeletal remains that are found around the lake region.

Dronagiri Trek

India has the largest conglomeration of peaks in the Himalayas. If Nanda devi, Neelkanth, Panchachuli and many more peaks are known for their towering heights there is a lone mountain called Dronagiri which is known for its closeness to the nation’s most reverred epic – The Ramayana. Everest in Nepal is the world’s highest peak and there is no village around it bearing the same name. Nanda Devi is the third highest peak in India without a village of its name. In fact none of the peaks have a village of the same name except Dronagiri peak, which has lent its name to a village in the vicinity.

Kuwari Pass Trek

Lord Curzon Trail is situated at an altitude of 4156 mt above sea level in Chamoli district of Uttarakhand, also known as a Kuari pass trek which is passes via famous snow sports destination Auli. This trek is KNOW as Lord Curzon Trail because Lord Curzon discovered this trail in 1905, after that many trekkers continuously visit this trek every year. Kuari pass connect two valleys of CHAMOLI GHARHWAL that is Joshimath & Ghat, also, offers mesmerizing views of some majestic peaks of Himalayas like Nanda Devi (7817 m), Kamet (7756m), Dronagiri (7066 m), Hathi Parvat (6727 m) & Ghori Parvat (6709 m).

Vashudhara Trek

Vasudhara Falls is a waterfall situated near Badrinath, in Uttarakhand, India. It is located on river "Alaknanda". Total distance from Badrinath to Vasudhara is 9 km. The first 3 kilometers of badrinath to mana village is drivable road. Next 6 km is walkable trek from Mana village to Vasudhara waterfall. The log inheight of this waterfall is 400 ft(122 mt)

Kakbhusandi Trek

Kagbhusandi lake is a small and beautiful lake situated in Hathi Parvat. It stands at an elevation of 5,230 meters above the sea level. Kankul Pass lies near this holy lake. The journey also allows you to get enchanted by the majestic view of the Neelkanth, Chaukhamba, and Nar-Narayan peaks.

It is surrounded by several major peaks of the Garhwal Himalayan region. From the group of 6thousanders, the Nilkantha at an elevation of 6,596 meters is the most prominent one. Madmaheshwar-Nandikund-Bansinarayan-Kalpeshwar Trek

This is one of the most enchanting treks in Uttarakhand Himalaya which takes you deep inside the greater Himalaya. Nandikund trek cross many rivulets, alpine meadows covered by innumerable and rare species of flowers and herbs, deep gorges, high snow clad peaks experiencing an imaginary, amazing and splendid view of the great mountains of Himalaya, and many other prominent places. Across this trek one comes across some wild animals like, Thar, Bharal, Musk deer and many more rare and endangered species as it comes under sanctuary and biosphere reserve area. This trek was used by PANDAVAS when they went in search of salvation to Heaven. Only few fortunate trekkers can experience the tranquillity and beauty of this trek due to its high terrain.

Rudranath Trek

Rudranath is the fourth of Panch Kedar circit. The temple is situated at an altitude of 3400 meters, and it takes 22 km or four days of trekking and pony rides to make a visit to this unique temple.

Chopta Camp

Chopta is a small region of meadows and evergreen forest area which is a part of kedarnath wildlife sanctuary located in Uttarakhand state, India and a base for trekking to Tungnath, third temple of Panch Kedar, which lies 3.5 kilometres (2.2 mi) away. Located at a distance of 1.5 kilometres (0.93 mi) from Tungnath is Chandrashila, a rising to over 4,000 metres (13,000 ft).

Anusuiya Camp

Anusuya Devi Temple, popularly referred to as the duo of Anusuya Devi Temple & Atri Muni Ashram is a major pilgrim center for Kedarnath travelers. The deity of Anusuya Devi Temple is Devi Sati, the fully dedicated wife of Atri Muni, one among the Sapta Rishis. A large festival is organized here every year in the month of December during the time of Duttatreya Jayanti, attended by hundreds of devotees and tourists. They will spent the entire night by worshipping Goddess Sati with lighted lamps in their hands and Bhajans on their lips. It is believed that Brahma, Vishnu and Maheshswara took the form of Anusya Devi’s sons and lived here. Anusuya Devi Temple is about 19 km away from Gopeshwar, 29 km from Chamoli, and 30 km from Chopta. The 6 km trekking to this place starts from Mandal, which is about 13 km from Gopeshwar. Atri Muni Ashram is located at a distance of 2 km from Anasuya Devi Temple.

Devriyataal Camp

Deoriat al Lake has captivating surroundings with Deodar, Oakwood and Rhododendron forest all around. Situated at about 2450 meters above sea level, there is an excellent camping ground near the lake that makes this destination a perfect place to spend a few camping days around nature. Reflection of mighty Chaukhamba Peak can be seen in the still water of this lake all day, given the weather is clear.
